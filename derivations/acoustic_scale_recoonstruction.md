- $c_s(z)$ is the sound speed of the photon-baryon plasma,
- and $H(z)$ is the cosmological expansion rate.

The sound speed satisfies:

$$
c_s(z) = \frac{c}{\sqrt{3(1+R(z))}}
$$

where the baryon-photon momentum-density ratio matches the engine configuration profile:

$$

The sound horizon therefore depends directly on:

- radiation evolution,
- and recombination-era plasma behavior.

$$
